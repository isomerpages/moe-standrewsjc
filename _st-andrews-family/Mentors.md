<p>The SAJC Mentors are members of the Chaplaincy team from Chapel of the
Resurrection (COR). We provide pastoral care to the SAJC Community. We
interact with students during daily school activities like PE lessons and
CCA sessions. We serve, help and guide Saints through life in college.</p>
