<p>Saint Andrew’s Junior College recognises students for <strong>academic excellence, character, leadership, service, and holistic development</strong> through
a range of awards and scholarships.</p>
<p>These awards affirm not just outcomes, but the <strong>values, growth, and contributions</strong> students
demonstrate throughout their time in college. All recipients are expected
to uphold <strong>good conduct</strong> and exemplify SAJC’s <strong>WISE &amp; TRUE values</strong>.</p>
<p>Students may be recognised through awards and scholarships that span:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Academic achievement and progress</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Leadership and service to the community</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Character and values-based contributions</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Co-curricular and talent development</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>National-level (MOE / Edusave) awards</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>Recognition may take place <strong>during the academic year</strong> or <strong>after the release of GCE A-Level results</strong>,
depending on the award. Awards and scholarships are conferred through different
processes, which may include:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Student applications</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Teacher nominations</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Student and teacher nominations</p>
<p></p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><em>For more details on the relevant Awards and Scholarships, students can refer to the Talent Development site on the Saints Portal.</em>
</p>
