<h2><u>By Public Transport:</u></h2>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Go to <strong>Potong Pasir MRT (NEL)</strong>
</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Use <strong>Exit C</strong>
</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Take <strong>Bus 142</strong> to bus stop <strong>61101</strong> at <strong>Blk 120 Potong Pasir Avenue 1</strong>
</p>
</li>
</ol>
<p></p>
<h2><u>By Car</u></h2>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Turn into Potong Pasir Avenue 1</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>At the first roundabout continue straight to stay on Potong Pasir Avenue
1</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>At the second roundabout, take the first exit through our main gates</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Continue straight past the St. Andrew's Hall towards the second guardhouse</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Follow the signs and drop off at the foyer at the end of the road</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Please be considerate and move to the end of the foyer to drop off so
that others can line up behind as well</p>
</li>
</ol>
<p><em>Note: There is limited parking on our campus. The closest public carpark is just outside the school at Blk 120 Potong Pasir Avenue 1. (Take the second exit at the second roundabout instead of driving through our main gates). You can enter the school by following the <u>park connector</u> right next to Blk 119 Potong Pasir Avenue 1.</em>
</p>
