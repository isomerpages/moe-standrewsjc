<h2><strong>SAJC JAE Appeal Exercise 2026</strong></h2>
<h3><strong>What is the JAE Appeal Exercise?</strong></h3>
<p>The JAE Appeal Exercise is for students who were <strong>not posted to St. Andrew's Junior College</strong> under
the Joint Admissions Exercise (JAE) and wish to appeal for admission.</p>
<hr>
<h3><strong>Application Period</strong></h3>
<p><strong>Start:</strong> Tuesday, 3 February 2026, 9:00 AM
<br><strong>End:</strong>  <strong>Monday, 9 February 2026</strong>, 12:00 Noon</p>
<p><em><sup>Edit: There was an error in the earlier update. Please note that the Appeal's deadline is 9th February, 12 noon.</sup></em>
</p>
<p>Late or incomplete applications will not be considered.</p>
<hr>

<p><strong>2. DSA Students</strong>
</p>
<p>Students admitted to their posted school via Direct School Admission (DSA)
are <strong>not eligible</strong> for this exercise, as DSA transfers are
not permitted.</p>

<p><strong>Application Outcome</strong>
</p>
<p><strong>Notification:</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p><strong>Successful applicants only</strong> will be notified via email
and/or phone call <strong>by Monday, 9 February 2026, 3:00 PM.</strong>
</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>If you do not receive notification by this time, your appeal has been
unsuccessful.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>For Successful Applicants:</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>You must visit SAJC <strong>in person</strong> to collect your transfer
letter.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<hr>
<p><strong>IMPORTANT: Action Required While Awaiting Results</strong>
</p>
<p><strong>All applicants must report to the institution posted by MOE</strong> or
accept your offered course online while waiting for your appeal outcome. <strong>Do not delay your registration at your posted school.</strong>
</p>

<p><strong>Please Note</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>The college's decision is <strong>final.</strong>
</p>
</li>
<li>

reasons for non-selection.</p>
</li>
