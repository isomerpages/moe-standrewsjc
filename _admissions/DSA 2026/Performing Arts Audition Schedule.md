<h4>Notes:</h4>
<p>There will be an audition and interview. The audition will take the form
of a rehearsal, during which, the applicants will:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Join us in our band practice (where they will play warm up chorales and
scales 5#’s, 5b’s in concert and instrument’s key);</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Have sectionals along with their seniors (where they will sight read a
piece, given on the spot by the Section Leaders);</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Perform one choice piece that will showcase a range of their techniques;
and</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Play games with the band.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<h4>Additional Information</h4>
<p>Please fill in the <strong><u>SAJC DSA Application Form</u></strong>. Do
note that only applicants who have submitted their supporting documents
will be considered for the audition and interview.</p>
<p><strong><u>You will be informed if you are shortlisted for the audition and interview, via your MOE student ICON email.</u></strong> Please <strong><u>do not</u></strong> come
down for the audition and interview if you have not received the email
informing that you are shortlisted.</p>
<p></p>
<p>If you have been invited for the audition and interview,</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Please come in your secondary school uniform; and</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Instruments may be made available upon request. Applicants need to bring
their own mouthpieces.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>Do contact the teachers-in-charge if there are any queries:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Ms Jesulyn Lim (<a href="mailto:lim_shi_en_jesulyn@moe.edu.sg" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">lim_shi_en_jesulyn@moe.edu.sg</a>)</p>

<h4>Notes</h4>
<p>The audition will include <strong>three</strong> components:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Applicants will have 20 min to interpret an unseen soliloquy <em><u>or</u></em> outline
how they intend to stage a piece of work;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Applicants will then &nbsp;take place in the form of a rehearsal, where
applicants can expect to participate in improvisation activities and games
alongside current members of the Drama Club.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Thereafter, a group interview will be conducted.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><strong>It is preferred that applicants have:&nbsp;</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Represented school in Singapore Youth Festival Arts Presentation</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Achievement/Awards in Singapore Youth Festival Arts Presentation</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Acting and/ or staging experiences outside the Singapore Youth Festival
Arts Presentation</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Candidates who have acting or crew experience in TV, video, film or professional
theatre companies, such as Act 3, will also be considered.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h4>Additional Information:</h4>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Only applicants who have submitted their supporting documents will be
considered.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Please note that only shortlisted applicants will be invited for an audition
and interview.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>If shortlisted, please come for the audition and interview in your secondary
school PE attire.</p>
</li>
</ul>
