<h1><strong>Direct School Admission (DSA) - 2027 Intake</strong></h1>
<p>St Andrew’s Junior College (SAJC) invites Secondary 4 students and their
equivalents from Singapore mainstream schools to apply for admission to
our Junior College programme commencing in 2027.&nbsp;&nbsp; DSA offers
talented students the opportunity to secure a place at SAJC before GCE
'O' Level results are released.
<br>
<br>Successful applicants must meet&nbsp;<a href="https://www.moe.gov.sg/post-secondary/admissions/jae/junior-colleges-and-millennia-institute" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">MOE's eligibility criteria</a>&nbsp;for
the two-year Junior College course, which is assessed primarily on GCE
'O' Level examination results.&nbsp; For full details on the DSA-JC exercise,
visit the&nbsp;<a href="https://www.moe.gov.sg/post-secondary/admissions/dsa" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">MOE DSA-JC website.</a>
</p>
<p></p>

<h2><strong>Key dates</strong></h2>
<h3>Applications open: <u>5 May 2026, 9:00 am</u></h3>
<p>The online application portal opens. You may begin submitting your application
with all required documents.</p>
<h3>Phase 1 deadline: <u>22 July 2026, 3:00 pm</u></h3>
<p>Closing date for Phase 1 applications. Applications received after this
time will not be considered for Phase 1.</p>
<h3>Phase 2 deadline: <u>19 August 2026, 3:00 pm</u></h3>
<p>Closing date for Phase 2 applications. Phase 1 applicants who were not
offered a place are automatically considered — no resubmission required.</p>
<p><strong><u>Missing a deadline</u></strong>: Late submissions will <strong><u>not</u></strong> be
considered. Please ensure that all documents are ready before submitting
your application.</p>

<h2><strong>Accepting an offer — what you need to know</strong></h2>
<p>Once you have accepted SAJC's DSA Offer, you will not be eligible to participate
in the Joint Admissions Exercise (JAE), as long as you meet the eligibility
criteria for JC admission based on your GCE O-Level examination results.</p>
<p>You are also not allowed to transfer to another school after the release
of the GCE O-Level examination results. You are expected to honour your
commitment to the college and your chosen CCA for the duration of your
studies in SAJC.</p>
<p>Students who do not fulfil their DSA commitment without valid reason may
be required to transfer out to another school.</p>
<p>In addition, applicant’s parents have to acknowledge, understand and agree
to the following:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>The Government of Singapore may collect, use, and disclose any personal
data which I have provided in this application for the purpose of facilitating
my child’s junior college arrangement and where my child is allocated to
a DSA-JC school, for administering and managing my child’s relationship
with that school and the Ministry of Education.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Once my child is successfully allocated to a DSA-JC school, he/she will
not be able to participate in the annual Joint Admissions Exercise (JAE)
to opt for another school/course.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>My child is required to honour his/her commitment to the allocated DSA-JC
school for the duration of the JC course.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>I undertake not to transfer my child to another school after the release
of the O Level results.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Should the other parent contest my child’s allocation to a DSA-JC school,
I undertake to resolve the issue with him/her directly.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>All the information provided by me in this application is true and correct.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>The Ministry of Education is entitled to remove my child from the allocated
DSA-JC school and enroll my child in any school as may be determined by
the Ministry of Education in its sole discretion if:</p>
<p>a. I have provided false information in this application;</p>
<p>b. I am unable to resolve any dispute with the child’s other parent with
regard to my child’s choice of school as stated above; or</p>
<p>c. Any person (including me or the other parent or any other person connected
with my child) has offered or given or agreed to give to any other person
any gift or consideration of any kind as an inducement or reward for showing
favour or disfavour to any person in relation to my child’s DSA application.</p>
</li>
</ul>
