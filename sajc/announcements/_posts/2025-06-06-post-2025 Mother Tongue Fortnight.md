<h2><strong>Embracing the spirit of having joy in learning</strong></h2>
<p>From 6th to 19th May, the spirit of unity and diversity came alive at
St. Andrew’s Junior College (SAJC) during our annual Mother Tongue Fortnight
(MTF) in conjunction with SG60. This vibrant two-week celebration brought
together students and staff in a dynamic showcase of our multicultural
heritage, making this year’s MTF the most memorable yet!</p>

<p>2025 saw the introduction of four exciting new programmes:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p><strong>Escape Room:</strong>&nbsp;Students raced against time, solving
language-based puzzles in a thrilling test of teamwork and wit.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Xin Yao Performances:</strong>&nbsp;The campus resonated with
nostalgic melodies, as students and guest performers serenaded the crowd
with beloved local tunes.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>China Immersion Trip Showcase:</strong>&nbsp;Participants shared
their eye-opening experiences, offering a window into Chinese culture and
language immersion.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Youth Bilingual Discourse (YBD):</strong>&nbsp;A national initiative
that sparked lively bilingual debates, fostering curiosity, collaboration,
and lifelong learning among JCCI students.</p>
</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Celebrating Culture: Music, Games, and Craftsmanship</strong>
</p>
<p>The festivities began each morning with&nbsp;<strong>AM Delight</strong>,
where students took on the role of radio deejays, spinning multicultural
tracks and sharing uplifting stories. The campus buzzed with the&nbsp;<strong>Busking Programme</strong>,
featuring student performances of ethnic songs during rest breaks, and
reached a crescendo with the national&nbsp;<strong>Xin Yao Programme</strong>.
This programme was featured in the Chinese newspapaper – Lianhe Zaobao
on 28<sup>th</sup> May 2025. Read more about it <a href="https://www.zaobao.com.sg/lifestyle/gen/story20250528-6475607" rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank">here</a>!</p>

<p>Traditional games like&nbsp;<strong>Chapteh</strong>,&nbsp;<strong>Congkak</strong>,
and&nbsp;<strong>Kongming Lock</strong>&nbsp;brought laughter and friendly
competition, while the&nbsp;<strong>Chinese Language Escape Room</strong>&nbsp;challenged
students’ problem-solving skills.</p>
<p><strong>Deepening Engagement: Master Classes and Symposiums</strong>
</p>
<p>MTF 2025 offered students the chance to deepen their appreciation for
language and culture through:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p><strong>Master Classes</strong>&nbsp;with experts in language, literature,
and culture.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>The&nbsp;<strong>Mother Tongue Language Symposium</strong>, where local
experts and student presenters discussed the tangible and intangible benefits
of mother tongue learning.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Learning Journeys</strong>&nbsp;that took students beyond the
classroom to experience the living heritage of our languages.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Competitions, Awards &amp; Recognitions</strong>
</p>
<p>No celebration is complete without a little friendly rivalry! Students
competed in deejay hosting, busking, and TikTok video creation. Outstanding
achievements were celebrated:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p><strong>Zhang Ruowei (25S02)</strong>&nbsp;clinched Excellent Awards in
two national Chinese calligraphy competitions.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>SAJC</strong>&nbsp;was honoured with the&nbsp;<strong>Harmony Award</strong>&nbsp;for
active participation in national calligraphy events.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Thashreefah Nasreen Binte Mohamed Eusoph (25A02)</strong>&nbsp;and&nbsp;<strong>Irba Qahirah Putri Mohamad Isa (24A05)</strong>&nbsp;were
appointed&nbsp;<strong>Rakan Bahasa</strong>&nbsp;(Friends of the Malay
Language) for their dedication to promoting Malay language and culture.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Growing Together: The SAJC Way</strong>
</p>
<p>This event reflects SAJC’s commitment to nurture both individual and collective
identities, preparing students to thrive in a multicultural society.<strong>:</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Embracing and celebrating diversity</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Fostering unity and social cohesion</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Promoting bilingualism and cultural heritage</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Encouraging holistic, experiential, and lifelong learning</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Building a vibrant, inclusive, and forward-looking community</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>MTF 2025 proved once again that at SAJC, we don’t just celebrate our roots
- we grow together, learn together, and create unforgettable memories.
The fortnight’s success is a testament to our commitment to holistic education
and the joy of learning.</p>
