<p>On 27 February 2026, the College marked a significant milestone as the
Class of 2025 received their results for the 2025 GCE A Level Examination.</p>
<p></p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/Announcements/2026 A Level Results Release/CAY_4974.jpg">
</div>
<p>The A Level journey represents not only academic rigour, but also resilience,
growth and the steady pursuit of excellence. Over the past two years, our
students have navigated demanding curricula, embraced co-curricular commitments,
and supported one another through both challenges and triumphs. Their results
reflect sustained effort, disciplined preparation and the collective strength
of the cohort.</p>
<p></p>
<p>As the Class of 2025 moves forward to diverse pathways, we remain confident
in their readiness to contribute thoughtfully and purposefully to society.
The experiences, values and relationships forged during their time at the
College will continue to guide them in the years ahead.</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/Announcements/2026 A Level Results Release/A_Level_Results_Release_2026.png">
</div>
<p></p>
<p>We extend our heartfelt congratulations to every member of the Class of
2025. This achievement marks both a culmination and a beginning. We look
forward to witnessing the many ways in which they will shape their future
and make a positive impact in the world.</p>
