<h2><strong>Cultivating Global Friendships: A Warm Welcome to Tsung Tsin Christian Academy</strong></h2>
<p>On 30 March 2026, St Andrew’s Junior College (SAJC) was delighted to host
160 students and 15 staff members from Tsung Tsin Christian Academy (TTCA),
Hong Kong. Led by their Principal, Mr. Cheung Man Wai, and Deputy Principal,
Mr. Cheung Chit, the visit was a wonderful opportunity to strengthen international
ties and share the Saint Andrew's spirit.</p>

<p>The day began with an assembly introduction and a meaningful exchange
of tokens of friendship. Our visitors were then immersed in the daily life
of a Saint:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p><strong>In the Classroom (Head):</strong> TTCA students joined Mr. Ma Huaqi’s
General Paper class (26S08), experiencing our collaborative and reflective
learning environment firsthand.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Around the Campus (Heart):</strong> Student hosts from our Outdoor
Activities Club (ODAC) led our guests on a campus tour, sharing stories
of our traditions and culture while building new bonds of friendship.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Leadership in Action (Hand):</strong> Our ODAC students, supported
by teachers Mr. Yeo Chee Keong, Ms. Peck Hwee Fang, Ms. Joy Tan, and Mr.
Gabriel Tang, stepped up as ambassadors of our "One Family Unbroken" spirit.</p>
</li>
</ul>
