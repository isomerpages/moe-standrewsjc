<p>The St Andrew’s Junior College community mourns the passing of our founding
Principal, Bishop John Tan Teng Wai, who returned to the Lord on 3 April.</p>
<p>As the pioneer leader appointed to start the College in 1978, Bishop Tan
oversaw the admission of our first 600 students at the Malan Road campus.
His leadership was defined by a remarkable resilience; despite starting
in premises that were not yet fully completed, he and his staff ensured
that the educational mission moved forward without pause.</p>
<p>Under his guidance, the College established a strong reputation for academic
excellence in the Commerce Faculty and a vibrant culture in sports and
track and field. Bishop Tan was a man of diverse passions: a leader who
championed student athletes and a musician who was well known for performing
on his saxophone within the college.</p>
<p>Above all, he was a man of deep faith who emphasised character. He frequently
shared Psalm 1 with his students, encouraging them to be like trees planted
by streams of water, rooted in what is right and honourable. We are deeply
thankful for the dedication and vision Bishop Tan provided during those
foundational years.</p>
<p>Our deepest condolences to his family and the many lives he touched. Rest
in peace, Bishop Tan. Up and On!</p>
