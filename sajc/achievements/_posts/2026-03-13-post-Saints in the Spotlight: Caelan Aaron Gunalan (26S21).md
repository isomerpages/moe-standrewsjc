<p>The College is proud to announce that Caelan Aaron Gunalan (26S21) has
been formally recognised by the <strong>Singapore Police Force (SPF)</strong> for
his quick thinking and public-spiritedness.</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 45%;" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/Announcements/2026 SPF Cert of Appreciation/Image_1___Receipt_of_Award.jpg">
</div>
<p>On 31 January 2026, Caelan observed a toddler wandering unattended toward
an open carpark at an HDB void deck. Sensing the immediate risk from oncoming
traffic, he acted decisively to bring the child to safety. He further assisted
the Neighbourhood Police Center by providing essential information that
facilitated a reunion between the child and his family.</p>
<p>For his selfless actions, Caelan was presented with a <strong>Certificate of Appreciation</strong> at
the Jurong West Neighbourhood Police Centre, marking his significant contribution
to community safety.</p>
<p>Caelan’s actions demonstrate what it means to be a community builder,
and what it means to be a leader who dares to act. We celebrate his alertness
and the exemplary character he displayed in a tense situation. Well done,
Caelan, we are proud of you!</p>
