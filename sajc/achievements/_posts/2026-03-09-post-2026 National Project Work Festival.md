<p>On Monday, 9 March, the Ministry of Education organised the inaugural <strong>National Project Work (PW) Festival</strong>,
a platform celebrating innovation and critical inquiry among JC students.
SAJC was well-represented by teams who demonstrated not just academic rigour,
but desire to serve their community through their project ideas.</p>

project ideas aimed at addressing societal challenges.</p>

<p>Reflecting on the journey, <strong>Asher</strong> shared:</p>
<blockquote>
<p><em>I appreciated how PW gave us the opportunity to explore areas that interested us. Being able to research a real-world issue and propose solutions made the learning experience more meaningful. <h2><strong>Marketplace of Ideas Pitch: Courage Under Pressure</strong></h2>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 75%;" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/Announcements/2026 Natl PW Fest/Picture4.png">
</div>
<p>In the competitive <strong>Marketplace of Ideas Pitch</strong>, SAJC stood
out as one of the few colleges with two teams shortlisted nationwide.</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Group C064: <strong>Aryan</strong> and <strong>Caleb</strong> pitched <strong>Making Jalan Besar More Besar</strong>.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Group C137: <strong>Jovia</strong> and <strong>Madelyn</strong> clinched <strong>4th Place</strong> nationally
with their project, <strong>Harmony4Kidz</strong>!</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/Announcements/2026 Natl PW Fest/Picture5.jpg">
</div>
<p>The pitch required <strong>Skilled Communication</strong> and <strong>Holistic Thinking</strong>,
qualities we strive to nurture in every Saint.</p>
<p>For <strong>Caleb</strong>, the experience was a lesson in resilience:</p>
<blockquote>
<p><em>Initially, I was quite averse to the idea of presenting to a panel from government organisations... But I overcame my nerves and stepped outside my comfort zone. I learnt presentation skills like body language and oratorical skills, as well as how to engage in a Q&amp;A effectively.</em>
</p>
</blockquote>
<p>We congratulate all our participants for representing the College with
such excellence and heart. The growth you have all shown is our greatest
achievement</p>
