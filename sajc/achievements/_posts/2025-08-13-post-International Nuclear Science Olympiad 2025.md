<p>From July 30 to August 6, several teams from across the globe took part
in the International Nuclear Science Olympiad. Out of a total of 8 Golds,
13 Silvers, ad 16 Bronzes given out at the Olympiad, Team Singapore clinched
4 Gold medals.</p>
<p>We are proud to announce that <strong>Phoon Jun Yan </strong>from <strong>24S20 </strong>was
one of the competitors to attain a <strong>Gold Medal</strong>. Since January
this year, Jun Yan had been attending weekly Physics Olympiad sessions
with Team Singapore. His achievements at the Olympiad show not only his
passion for science, but also the dedication and hard work he had put in.</p>
<p>Congratulations Team Singapore and Jun Yan - keep the flame burning brightly
ever!</p>
