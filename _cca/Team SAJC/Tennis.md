<p>The SAJC Tennis Boys’ and Girls’ teams are brought together by one thing,
a huge love for the game. Whether we’re smashing forehands or chasing down
impossible shots, we give our all on court. Training days are filled with
racquet drills, friendly match-ups, and plenty of laughs as we build both
our skills and our resilience.</p>
<p>We also bring that same fire to the annual National School Games, where
we play our hearts out and push ourselves to new heights.</p>
<p>Trainings are on Mondays, Wednesdays and Thursdays, and if you’ve got
the passion, drive, and a good vibe for tennis, we’d love to have you with
us. Come create some epic memories with the team!</p>
