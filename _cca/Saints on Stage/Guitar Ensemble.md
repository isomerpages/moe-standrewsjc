<p>Ever wanted to make music with others? The SAJC Guitar Ensemble brings
together guitarists of all levels to learn, perform, and build lasting
friendships through music.</p>
<p>Whether you're working on technique in sectional practices or performing
at our Guitar Festival, public performances, or the Singapore Youth Festival,
you'll develop your musicianship alongside a supportive community of fellow
guitarists. We're proud to maintain our track record of excellence, achieving
the <strong>Certificate of Distinction</strong> at the Singapore Youth Festival
Arts Presentation in 2021, 2023, and 2025.</p>
<p><strong>No experience needed!</strong> Our experienced members will guide
you from your very first chord. If you're passionate about music and eager
to learn, we'd love to have you join us.</p>
<p>Come meet us on <strong>Wednesdays and Fridays</strong> to learn more!</p>
