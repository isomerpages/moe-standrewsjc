<p>The SAJC Dance Society is a dynamic collective of passionate dancers,
united by a shared love for the art of movement. Through a diverse repertoire
spanning expressive contemporary works to high-energy hip-hop pieces, we
aim to captivate and inspire our audiences with every performance. Our
dancers regularly take the stage at major College events such as College
Day, Open House, and our annual Dance Concert, where they showcase both
technical excellence and strong artistic expression.</p>
<p>Our dedication to the craft is reflected in our consistent achievements
at the Singapore Youth Festival, where we have attained the Certificate
of Distinction consecutively from 2013 to 2023.</p>
<p>Thinking of dancing with us? CCA sessions are held every Tuesday and Wednesday
and we look forward to welcoming anyone with an interest in dance. No prior
dance experience needed!</p>
